Comprehensive Heart Care

Heart Care at SDP Clinic focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cardiovascular conditions. Our goal is to help patients maintain a healthy heart and reduce the risk of serious complications.

Cardiac Evaluation & Diagnosis +

Detailed cardiac evaluations to assess heart function, identify risks, and detect early signs of cardiovascular disease.

Blood Pressure Management +

Comprehensive care for high blood pressure through monitoring, medication management, and lifestyle guidance.

Cholesterol & Lipid Control +

Treatment plans to manage cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart attacks and other cardiac complications.

Heart Health Monitoring +

Ongoing monitoring of heart health to track progress, prevent deterioration, and support long-term wellness.

Lifestyle & Preventive Counseling +

Guidance on diet, exercise, stress management, and healthy habits to strengthen heart health and prevent future issues.

Preventing Complications

Our specialists are committed to early detection and effective treatment to reduce the risk of heart-related complications and promote long-term cardiovascular health.
